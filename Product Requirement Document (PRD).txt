Project Name: Food Stall Discovery Platform Version: 1.0 (Funded Vision / Indian Market Context)


Authors: Surena Shafizadeh & Team 
+1

1.1 Executive Summary
A dedicated mobile application and companion website connecting users with local street food stalls. The platform addresses the market gap for reliable, real-time information (open/closed status) and hygiene transparency in the unorganized street food sector.
+1

1.2 Objectives

Primary: To launch a localized digital platform connecting users with reliable, real-time food stall data.

Secondary:

Empower micro-entrepreneurs with simple digital tools for visibility.

Establish a trust layer regarding hygiene through owner transparency and user feedback.

Support deep localization (regional languages, cuisine specificity).

1.3 Target Audience

Users (Foodies): Students, office workers, and tourists aged 18-45 seeking authentic, affordable local food.


Stall Owners (Vendors): Micro-entrepreneurs, mobile cart owners, and pop-up vendors, often with limited digital literacy.
+1

1.4 Feature Requirements
A. User-Facing Features (MVP)


Discovery: Interactive map and list views showing stalls based on GPS location.

Advanced Search & Filter:

Filter by Cuisine (e.g., Chaat, Dosa), Dietary Tags (Jain, Halal, Vegan), and Price.
+1


Hygiene Indicators: Filter by "Owner-Verified Hygiene" or user hygiene ratings.


Real-Time Status: Prominent "Open Now" or "Closed" indicators updated live by owners.


Stall Profiles: High-quality photos, menu lists, and location pins.


Hygiene Review System: Structured review flow asking specifically about glove use, water source visibility, and cleanliness.

B. Stall Owner-Facing Features (MVP)


One-Tap Updates: Extremely simple interface to toggle "Open/Closed" status.
+1


Location Pinning: Mobile vendors can update their live GPS location with one tap.


Hygiene Showcase: Optional section to upload photos of water filters, gloves, or FSSAI registration.


Menu Management: Simple text inputs to update daily specials or out-of-stock items.

1.5 Success Metrics (KPIs)

Reliability: Frequency of owner "Open/Closed" updates.


Growth: DAU/MAU and Number of Registered Stalls.
+1


Trust: Percentage of reviews containing hygiene-specific feedback.
